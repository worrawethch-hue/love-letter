# LOVE LETTER
Version 1.0

---

# PROJECT PHILOSOPHY

This project is not a website.

It is an emotional experience.

The objective is not to impress with animation.

The objective is to make one person feel

"This was made especially for me."

Every design decision must support this feeling.

---

# CORE RULES

Always choose

Elegance > Fancy

Emotion > Feature

Quality > Quantity

Whitespace > Decoration

Story > Interaction

Never add features simply because they look cool.

---

# TARGET USER

One person.

Female.

Quiet personality.

Doesn't express emotions much.

Warm when close to someone.

Hard-working.

Likes subtle romance.

Dislikes exaggerated confession.

The experience should feel respectful, gentle and sincere.

---

# USER EXPERIENCE GOAL

The reader should never feel tricked.

Instead,

she should slowly realize

"This wasn't just a quiz..."

Everything should naturally transition into the letter.

Purpose

Build trust.

Looks like a real personality assessment.

Five sliders.

Words of Affirmation

Acts of Service

Receiving Gifts

Quality Time

Physical Touch

Each slider

0-100%

Live percentage.

Continue button disabled until every slider has been touched.

# SCENE 2

Purpose

Increase curiosity.

Analyzing...

Preparing...

100%

Pause

---

# SCENE 3

Purpose

Reveal intention.

Text appears one paragraph at a time.

Actually...

We simply wanted a beautiful way to invite you here.

---

# SCENE 4

Purpose

Transition into letter.

Large envelope.

Wax seal.

Click seal.

Seal breaks.

Envelope opens.

Letter slowly slides upward.

---

# SCENE 5

Purpose

Express feelings.

Each screen contains only ONE idea.

Maximum 60 words.

Whitespace is more important than text.

---

# SCENE 6

Purpose

No fireworks.

"I like you."

Silence.

Pause.

Continue.

# SCENE 7

# SCENE 8
