# TASK_004

## Title

Envelope Transition

---

## Objective

Create a calm transition from the reveal into the letter.

The envelope should feel elegant and meaningful.

Do NOT display the letter yet.

---

## Story

After Scene 3 fades out

↓

Pause about 1.5 seconds

↓

A sealed paper envelope slowly rises from the bottom.

↓

The envelope gently floats into the center.

↓

Pause.

↓

The envelope slowly opens by itself.

↓

Soft warm light appears inside.

↓

Fade to white.

Stop here.

---

## Design

Minimal.

Cream paper.

Elegant shadow.

No icons.

No emojis.

Premium wedding invitation style.

---

## Animation

Slow.

Natural.

Soft easing.

No bouncing.

---

## Interaction

No buttons.

No clicking.

Everything plays automatically.

- index.html

## Do Not

Do not reveal feelings.
