## Improve Envelope Visual Design

### Goal

Improve the appearance of the envelope only.

Keep the same animation, timing, and scene flow.

---

## Requirements

- Keep the existing envelope animation.
- Keep the existing automatic transition.
- Do not change any timing.
- Do not modify any later scenes.

Improve only the visual appearance.

The envelope should:

- use a warm ivory / cream paper color
- clearly stand out from the background
- have a soft elegant shadow
- have a subtle paper-like feeling
- have a thin elegant border
- have a gentle warm glow while opening
- feel like a premium handwritten invitation

The background should remain soft and warm.

Avoid:

- dark brown envelopes
- muddy colors
- heavy shadows
- cartoon appearance
- bright yellow gold

Everything should feel calm, minimal, elegant, and premium.
